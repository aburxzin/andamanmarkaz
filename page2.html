MARKAZ FAZLE HAQ ACADEMY

Markaz Fazle Haq Academy, non-governmental and philanthropic Institute. This institute name after the most influential religious scholar and freedom fighter Al Sheikh Hassanul Hind Allama Fazle Haq Khairabadi(r), who is the torch bearer of this island.The institution has stretched and expanded over the years with the spiritual presence of reputed Islamic figure Al Sheikh Hassanul Hind Allama Fazle Haq Khairabadi. Markaz Fazle Haq Academy, popularly known as Andaman Markaz is one of the outstanding and commendable ventures providing a distinct blend of spiritual and temporal learning. It aims to mould a group equipped with knowledge to steer Islamic pulses of a new era. Religious scholars equally competent in worldly affairs are the need of the era. Along with strong emphasis on theology and spirituality, ample exposure to updated information, latest technical knowledge & modern trends in education are provided.

MISSION

To metamorphose into a unique Centre of Excellence, where, a cadre of spiritually holistic, professionally competent, intellectually vibrant scholars are moulded & groomed, to meet the challenges of future.

VISION

Creation of an excellent educational environment on par with global standards in the sacred Islamic and secular institutional realms of learning, keeping pace with the significant changes happening in the structures of the society.

OUR INSTITUTIONS

COLLEGE OF INTERGRATED STUDIES

The College of Integrated studies is an Institution that provides in-depth training in Islamic education as well as great Islamic scholars. Islamic literature, Islamic Jurisprudence, Theology, Logic, Sufi Literature, Astronomy, Arabic Literature. Special coaching to become a Lawyer, Research Scholar, PhD, Civil Servants etc are also being provided to the students. Boys who have passed the seventh grade are admitted. After the eight year integrated curriculum, guidance for PG, PhD and more.

MARKAZ PUBLIC SCHOOL

MPS Andaman is an autonomous centre under JamiaMarkaz (Kerala) which has a number of educational institutions under its patronage. The main objective of the education provided by Markaz Public School is to fulfil all the needs of education for the all-round development of the learners. Our mission is to achieve our vision of education in an environment of spirituality, love and kindness which provides the best of education in formative years by blending "spiritualism" with "modernism" and using modern technological tools, taught by highly qualified faculty to deliver.

SCHOOL OF QURAN

The School of Quran put forward the idea of presenting the old system to society in a new way.Schools of Quran offer a specialized educational environment where students begin their Quranic studies from the 6th grade.Students get to learn Hifz-ul-Quran through audio-visuals and get secondary education in English. Besides, enhance the possibility of learning Hifz in different Islamic countries around the world, and also provide job opportunities outside the country

ZeeQue PRESCHOOL

ZeeQue Preschool is a unique and state of the art Early Childhood Care and Education program specially designed for children between 3 to 6 years of age, spanning three developmental stages: Lower, Middle, and Upper (LZQ, MZQ, and UZQ). Our preschool is founded on the principle of helping the future generation shape their lives in accordance with Islamic values, facilitated through child-friendly mentoring

MARKAZ OCEAN PEARL

A shelter for orphans and destitute childrens, Markaz makes the orphans an integral part of society and creates good citizens. Children of age from 6 to 9 STD can come and get religious education along with the best of moral education. We provide for all of their requirements, including clothing, education, and medical supplies. And after completing a higher degree, Markaz assists the orphans in finding good careers.
